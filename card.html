Lion

Lions are symbols of courage and strength and have been celebrated in the past for these valiant characteristics.

- MomJunction.com

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Weight: 190-550 kg
  • Lifespan: 10-14 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands and savannahs

Despite being popularly known as the "king of the jungle", the lion is an animal of the open plains, and can be found throughout Africa. It is nevertheless a threatened species with significant populations being limited to national parks in Tanzania and South Africa. Before the rise of man, the species existed on more territory than any other land mammal.
